I am a software engineer at Google working on mobile computational photography with the Android Camera Algorithms team!

Previously, I was a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Keenan Crane. As a member of the CMU Graphics Lab and the CMU Geometry Collective, I researched interesting problems in computer graphics and geometry processing. This culminated in my Master’s Thesis on remeshing geometric surfaces.

I also completed my undergraduate studies at Carnegie Mellon, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Robotics. Outside of work, I enjoy watching movies, playing video/boardgames with friends, and listening to music (long-time swiftie). Also a fan of traveling, sports, and good food :)
